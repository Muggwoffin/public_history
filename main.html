ABOUT

I am an Irish historian based in Belfast, where my AHRC-funded project explores an anti-Nazi resistance movement that operated out of a series of vegetarian restaurants. My broad work covers many fields, with my publications touching on topics as varied as British surveillance of travelling revolutionaries, the cross-currents of early sexology and esoteric religion, and historical video games.
