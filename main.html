I

About

Historian of Revolutionary Life

I am an Irish historian based in Belfast, where I am an AHRC-funded Research Fellow at Queen's University. My work traces how early twentieth century revolutionaries navigated the messiness of day-to-day life. This has led me to explore topics as varied as the anti-Nazi underground, Irish nationalism, the Comintern and queer sexuality.
